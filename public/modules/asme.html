Plot Twist

Participants will be provided with situations where some basic concepts will be missing or a bit altered. They have to come up with ideas to understand and work in such a situation. They need to write a detailed description of their situation individually. They have to include the effect of the changes provided to them in the problem statement and also describe their method to work in the situation.

Mascot Making

A mascot is a person, animal, or object adopted by a group as a symbolic figure. The main purpose of this event is to identify and build a strong brand identity. The best mascot design chosen will be the official mascot of ASME NITS Chapter for the ongoing year. In this way, ASME NITS Community can have a theme with which we can move forward.

DRAFT RITZ 4.0

Participants will be provided with a problem statement on the day of the event and they need to provide solutions to that problem statement in the form of tool or machine prototype within 3 hours from the announcement of the problem statement. Participants need to give the solution in pdf format including a detailed explanation of the tool or machine prototype along with a design of the prototype in Solidworks (or any open source software) format or a drawing description of the prototype with at least the required views.

PITCH THE CAR

Sometimes only your technical knowledge and skills don't take you to a finish line in a race , it is even your presentation skills and the way you grab and impress people towards your knowledge. Technoeisis'21 under ASME module presents to you "pitch the car". Present your technical skills of your favorite automobile and prove that it's the cream of the crop and reach the finish line. During the competition each team will be provided with 7-10 min to pitch their car via Presntation they make. The best team whoever will pitch the best by explaining the real features of the car wisely by using all their technical and marketing skills wins.
